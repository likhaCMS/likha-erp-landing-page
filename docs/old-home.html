Digital Approvals & Shop Floor IoT

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to sign a long-term 12-month contract?

🚫 No long-term trap: We don’t believe in locking you into a long-term SaaS contract.
⏱️ The requirement: Because successful ERP implementation requires deep workflow customization, testing, and team training, we simply require a **3-Month Minimum Build Phase** on one of our Cloud plans.
🕊️ The freedom: After those first 90 days, you are strictly month-to-month and free to transfer your finalized system On-Premise at any time.

Why do I have to start on the Cloud? Can’t I just buy the On-Premise system immediately?

🎯 We don't build blind: We don't want you to pay for a system that doesn't perfectly fit your factory.
🛠️ Your active workshop: The Cloud phase allows you to test the live prototype, tweak workflows, and perfect the system in real-time.
🚀 Flawless deployment: Once we know the system operates flawlessly for your specific needs, then we deploy the finalized version to your local server.

What does "Operational Use Only" mean for the On-Prem Transfer?

To guarantee absolute stability and security on your local servers, the On-Premise deployment is locked to operational roles:
✅ What you CAN do: Your team can manage daily data, process Job Orders, add users, and run reports.
❌ What is LOCKED: The core system architecture (database structures, core workflows, and module logic) cannot be altered. This prevents accidental system breaks and ensures your factory runs without downtime.

What if my business grows and I need to add new features after moving On-Premise?

You simply remodel your system using our "Rent-to-Own" loop! Here is how the upgrade cycle works:
1.  Reactivate: 🔄 Start up a Likha ERP Cloud plan to unlock your Builder Access.
2. Build & Test: 🏗️ We use the Cloud environment to safely build and test your new features without interrupting your live factory floor.
3. Redeploy: 💻 Once the updates are perfect, the standard **₱30k On-Premise Transfer fee** applies to securely push your newly upgraded architecture back to your local server.

Who owns the data if I move to my own server?

🤝 You do. 100%. When you pay the ₱30k On-Premise Transfer fee, your complete system and all your historical data are securely migrated to your private local servers. You own the asset, and you control the access.

What kind of support do I get on the On-Premise plan?

✅ Included: Vital support for critical bugs and security patches to keep your system safe.
⛔ Not included: Because you are no longer paying a monthly management fee, structural customizations, workflow consulting, and hands-on system building are reserved for active Cloud subscribers.
