Who We Are

ZyberLab Solution Inc. has been a trusted provider of IT training and software development services since 2015, originally operating under the name Crown Byte.

Recognizing the growing demand for high-quality tech solutions, the co-founders officially registered the business with the DTI in 2021 under the trade name Zyberlab IT Solutions. This milestone solidified the company’s commitment to delivering innovative software solutions and empowering individuals through hands-on IT training programs.

In February 2026, driven by continuous growth and a vision to scale, the company transitioned from a DTI-registered business to a fully incorporated entity, officially registering with the SEC as ZyberLab Solution Inc.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To build a self-sustaining digital ecosystem in the Philippines where homegrown technology and top-tier local IT talent continuously drive MSME growth and national economic progress.

Mission: To empower Philippine businesses and tech professionals by bridging the gap between education and enterprise. We deploy Likha ERP to build resilient digital infrastructures, while delivering transformative IT training to cultivate the nation’s next generation of software engineers.

Our Core Goals:
1. Empower MSMEs: Align with the DOST Wealth Pillar by giving growing businesses the tools they need to scale without the burden of expensive, global software subscriptions.
2. Elevate Education: Transform students into proven engineers through real-world projects and rigorous IT training programs.
3. Champion Innovation: Fulfill UN SDG #9 by ensuring no local business is left behind, actively building the industry and infrastructure of tomorrow.

LikhaERP Roadmap 

6-step path to permanent system ownership — stop renting, start owning

Step 1

Discovery (Site Visit)

Trace operations, find pain points like pilferage and errors; deliver Business Process Map. Cost: ₱0.00

Step 2

Blueprint (Architect's Design)

This is a timeline block. Click on the blue gear icon to set the number of items in this timeline. All sites you create with the Mobirise web builder are mobile-friendly natively. No special actions required.

Step 3

Proposal (Bill of Materials)

Foundation ₱30k. Custom blocks: Forms ₱4k, Panels ₱2.5k, Roles ₱6.5k, APIs ₱15k. Deployment: Cloud ₱5k–20k/mo or On-Premise ₱50k–120k.

Step 4

Construction (Build)

Kickoff with ₱30k Acceptance Fee; deploy private server and build forms and automations as designed.

Step 5

Test and Train (Walkthrough)

Prototype testing with real data; tweak layouts. Training for Admin, Warehouse, and Sales staff.

Step 6

Turnover (Keys to the House)

System becomes your permanent asset. Support: Standard included, Pro ₱5k/mo, Enterprise ₱20k/mo.
